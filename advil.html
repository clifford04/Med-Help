Advil


Effects: Pain reliever and an anti-inflammatory medication, reducing swelling.


Side Effects: Nausea, heartburn, stomach pain, dizziness, high blood pressure, kidney damage, stomach ulcers/bleeding, or increased risk of heart attacks


Alternatives: Omega-3 EFAs(Fish Oil), Turmerie, Green Tea, and more


Dosage: 1 Capsule every 4-6 hours
